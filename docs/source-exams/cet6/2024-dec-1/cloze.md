# CET-6 2024-dec-1 Section A Cloze

## Passage

With the rapid progress in the economy, it is natural that people’s ideology is bound for change. Being [26] is no longer a virtue to some people, and they are not alone in holding this view. Each might have different viewpoints on how to lead one’s lifestyle: some prefer simplicity, some love luxuries, some spend [27], Some accumulate as much as they can like a country mouse. As I was born poor and in the countryside, the simple rural life seldom loses its [28] to me, but as my station changes, I have an opportunity to [29] with the newly rich, an experience which has revolutionized my view of lifestyle.

It is evil or sinful to spend little and to save much, an idea only recently being revealed to me. Actually the whole world is in a [30] to borrow and to spend, with the USA leading the trend. The USA is still the richest and most powerful country in the world, but the [31] is that they borrow money from us. Though a [32] of economics, at least I know that investing with borrowed money will turn the borrower into the [33] rather than the decent and kind lender. An apparent case is real estate. Those who are [34] and have got loans from the bank become relatively rich by selling their purchased home at a price several folds higher. Even education is not immune from this theory. Many of those poor parents who have the vision to send their children to college have benefited from this investment. even though they have to [35] a living. It is reasonable to conclude that spending is praise worthy, supposing it is not beyond your means.

## Explanations

26:解析：Being thrifty意为“节俭”，与后文“不再是美德”的语境呼应，thrifty（节俭的）符合语义，故选O。
27:解析：spend extravagantly意为“挥霍无度”，extravagantly（奢侈地）与前文“喜欢奢华”的生活方式对应，故选B。
28:解析：loses its appeal to me意为“对我失去吸引力”，appeal（吸引力）符合乡村生活对作者的影响，故选A。
29:解析：mingle with the newly rich意为“与新富阶层交往”，mingle（交往、混在一起）符合语境，故选J。
30:解析：in a rage to borrow and spend意为“疯狂借贷消费”，rage（狂热、愤怒）形容全球消费热潮，故选L。
31:解析：the irony is that...意为“讽刺的是……”，irony（讽刺）符合美国虽富有却向他国借钱的反差，故选D。
32:解析：a layman of economics意为“经济学门外汉”，layman（外行）符合作者自谦的表述，故选E。
33:解析：turn the borrower into the predator意为“把借款人变成掠夺者”，predator（掠夺者）与“善良出借人”形成对比，故选K。
34:解析：Those who are shrewd意为“精明的人”，shrewd（精明的）符合靠贷款炒房致富的人群特征，故选N。
35:解析：scrape a living意为“勉强糊口”，scrape（勉强维持）符合贫困父母供孩子上大学的艰难处境，故选M。
