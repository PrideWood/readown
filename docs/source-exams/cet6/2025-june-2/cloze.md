## Passage

It can “[26] being frozen at -272° Celsius being exposed to the vacuum of outer space and even being [27] with 500 times the dose of X-rays that would kill a human. In other words, the creature can endure conditions that don’t even exist on Earth. And researchers are looking to the microscopic animals to learn how to prepare humans and crops to handle the [28] of space travel.

The tardigrade’s indestructibility stems from its [29] to its environment — which may seem surprising, since it lives in [30] comfortable places like the cool, wet patches of moss (青苔) that dot a garden wall.

## Explanations

26:解析：can后接动词原形，survive（存活、经受住）符合“在极端环境下存活”的语境，故选K。
27:解析：be blasted with X-rays为固定搭配，意为“被X射线照射”，符合语义，故选B。
28:解析：the rigors of space travel指“太空旅行的严酷考验”，rigors（严酷、严苛）符合语境，故选I。
29:解析：its adaptations to environment指“对环境的适应”，adaptations（适应）符合语义，故选A。
30:解析：seemingly comfortable places指“看似舒适的地方”，seemingly（看似、表面上）符合语境，故选J。
31:解析：干旱对多数生物是灾难性的，catastrophic（灾难性的）符合语义，故选C。
32:解析：have evolved special strategies指“进化出特殊策略”，evolved（进化出的）符合语境，故选D。
33:解析：fill the cell’s interior指“填充细胞内部”，interior（内部）符合语义，故选F。
34:解析：prevent...from unfolding指“防止展开/解体”，unfolding（展开、散开）符合语境，故选O。
35:解析：accomplish this feat指“完成这一壮举”，feat（壮举、功绩）符合语义，故选E。
